User Preferences and Model Selection

One of the strenghts of Civic Builder is the flexibility and convenience of multiple pre-trained detection models to suit your needs. Here is the suggested workflow:

  • • Analyze available detection models. Each model is specialized for certain regions of the world.
  • • By selecting the region most closely resembling your imagery, you can achieve better results in feature extractionl.
  • • Select from the dropdown your new model.

Detection Model Selection

Choose from the following detection models tailored for specific locations and imagery resolutions:

  • • Roboflow Default Model (Location: India, Low Resolution Imagery)
  • • Dubai Human in the Loop Model
  • • SpaceNet 2 Paris Model
  • • SpaceNet 2 Shanghai Model
  • • SpaceNet 2 Khartoum Model
  • • SpaceNet 2 Las Vegas Model

User Preferences

Customize your extraction features and post-processing preferences:

  • Feature to Extract: By default, extract building footprints. Optionally extract roads, trees, rivers, etc.
  • Post-Processing Algorithm: Select a post-processing algorithm to fine-tune your export results.
